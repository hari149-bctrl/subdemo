Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how BrainyVoyage collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Instagram automation services.

Key Point: We only collect data necessary to provide our services and never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Our application collects and processes the following information when you connect your Facebook/Instagram account:

  • Facebook Account Information: Your Facebook user ID, name, email address (if provided), and profile picture.
  • Page & Instagram Data: Information about your connected Facebook Pages and Instagram Business Accounts including page names, IDs, access tokens, and Instagram usernames.
  • Post & Comment Data: Content of your Instagram posts, captions, comments (including comment text, usernames, timestamps), and engagement metrics.
  • Settings Data: Your configured keywords, custom messages, and reply templates stored in our database.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide core functionality of automated comment responses
  • Send customized messages when keywords are detected
  • Manage your connected social media accounts
  • Improve our service and troubleshoot issues
  • Comply with legal requirements

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared with:

  • Meta Platforms: To authenticate and interact with Facebook/Instagram APIs
  • Service Providers: MongoDB for data storage, hosting providers
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Retention

  • User account data is retained until you delete your account
  • Instagram comments are automatically deleted after 7 days
  • Access tokens are stored until you revoke them via Facebook

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Disconnect your Facebook/Instagram integration
  • Opt out of data processing (though this will disable service functionality)

6. Security Measures

We implement:

  • Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Limited access tokens with only required permissions
  • Regular security audits
